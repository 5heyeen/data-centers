## Section 6: Implications for Metier

### 6.1 Nordic Market Entry Point
Ramboll has one strong Nordic hyperscaler reference (Meta Odense) and one strong Nordic telco reference (TeliaSonera Helsinki), both focused on **sustainability and district heating integration**. For Metier to compete or partner:
- On sustainability/waste heat projects: Ramboll is a strong engineering partner candidate
- On PM advisory: Metier has no direct Ramboll competition in the Nordics
- On technical design: Ramboll's EYP MCF + i3 combination is formidable

### 6.2 Hyperscaler Engagement
The inference is that EYP MCF has hyperscaler relationships but they are confidential. If Norwegian hyperscale projects (Google Skien, potential Microsoft/Meta expansion) come to market, Ramboll's EYP MCF heritage gives them credible positioning. Metier's competitive response would be to **differentiate on owner-side PM role** rather than compete on engineering capability.

### 6.3 Client Intelligence for Norwegian Operators
Norwegian operators (Green Mountain, Bulk Infrastructure, STACK/DigiPlex, Lefdal Mine) do not appear in Ramboll's public references. This suggests:
- These operators may use other engineering consultants (local or international)
- Opportunity for Metier to establish PM advisory relationships with these operators before Ramboll does
- The new Norwegian Data Centre Industry association may be an entry point for relationship-building

### 6.4 AI Campus Focus
The Texas AI campus win (Oct 2025) signals EYP MCF is actively expanding into AI infrastructure. If Norwegian AI campus opportunities emerge (e.g., Nordic HPC centers, AI training facilities leveraging renewable power), Ramboll/EYP MCF will be a credible bidder. Metier should **monitor for AI-specific Norwegian DC announcements and position early**.

# Prompt 03: ous-oslo-universitetssykehus

> **Prompt:** Research Oslo universitetssykehus (OUS) construction and consolidation projects — budgets, schedules, contract models, and delivery problems.

---

## Research Summary

OUS is Norway's most complex hospital consolidation programme — not a single project but a portfolio of six major building projects across multiple sites in Oslo, coordinated by Sykehusbygg HF on behalf of Helse Sør-Øst RHF.

### 1. Scope of OUS Construction Projects

"Totalt skal det bygges ca 305 000 kvm nye bygg med sykehusfunksjoner" (approximately 305,000 m² of new hospital buildings in total). — [Search snippet from Helse Sør-Øst / NTB press release, WebSearch 1, 2026-04-25]

The six projects within the OUS portfolio include:
- **Legevakten** (emergency outpatient clinic) — completed and taken into use summer 2023
- **Nytt klinikkbygg på Radiumhospitalet** (new cancer clinic) — taken into use 2024
- **Livsvitenskapsbygget** (life science building, shared with UiO) — planned 2026
- **Ny sikkerhetspsykiatri på Ila** (new forensic psychiatry facility) — clinical opening planned autumn 2026
- **Nye Aker sykehus** — planned completion 2031/32
- **Nye Rikshospitalet** — planned completion 2031/32

These last two (Nye Aker and Nye Rikshospitalet) represent the core of the Oslo hospital consolidation and are the megaproject elements at ~20B NOK scale each. — [Search snippet from oslo-universitetssykehus.no budget document, WebSearch 1, 2026-04-25]

### 3. Schedule Performance

The two major projects (Nye Aker, Nye Rikshospitalet) have planned completion in 2031/32. These are not yet completed, so schedule outturn cannot be assessed. The programme was formally approved by Helse Sør-Øst board in December 2022. — ["Helse Sør-Øst vedtok Nye Rikshospitalet og Nye Aker" — Uniforum, December 2022: https://www.uniforum.uio.no/nyheter/2022/12/helse-sor-ost-vedtok-nye-rikshospitalet-og-nye-ake.html — 403 on WebFetch]

### 4. Contract Models

A significant contract has already been placed: "AF Gruppen vinner milliardkontrakt på råbygg ved nye Rikshospitalet" — [Byggfakta Nyheter: https://byggfaktanyheter.no/af-gruppen-vinner-milliardkontrakt-pa-rabygg-ved-nye-rikshospitalet/ — 403 on WebFetch]

Helse Sør-Øst also confirmed signing a separate "milliardkontrakt på Rikshospitalet" — [https://www.helse-sorost.no/nyheter/inngar-milliardkontrakt-pa-rikshospitalet/ — 403 on WebFetch]

The Sykehusbygg informasjonsmøte page indicates a package-based procurement approach: "Nye Aker og Nye Rikshospitalet skal inngå kontrakter innenfor bygg og teknikk for 25 milliarder kroner. Det skal dessuten anskaffes utstyr for de to sykehusene for om lag to milliarder" — [Search snippet from Sykehusbygg HF informasjonsmøte page, WebSearch 3, 2026-04-25]

**Inferred:** The OUS megaprojects are being procured through multiple separate contracts (delte entrepriser / package approach) rather than a single totalentreprise covering both sites.
**Based on:** "kontrakter innenfor bygg og teknikk for 25 milliarder kroner" (plural "contracts") — [Sykehusbygg HF informasjonsmøte, WebSearch 3 snippet]; "AF Gruppen vinner milliardkontrakt på råbygg" (single structural works package, not whole hospital) — [Byggfakta Nyheter, WebSearch 3 snippet]
**Reasoning:** The references consistently use plural contracts and reference specific packages (råbygg/structural works, teknikk/MEP) rather than a single integrated contract, indicating a split-contract or managing contractor approach consistent with Norwegian public hospital procurement norms rather than a single totalentreprise.

## Inferred Findings

**Inferred:** The OUS megaproject programme is at high cost-escalation risk, having already seen estimates range from ~37B to 44.7B+ NOK since approval, suggesting scope or cost control challenges even in early phases.
**Based on:** "Samlet budsjettramme for de to nye sykehusene i Oslo er på 42 milliarder kroner" (January 2024) — [WebSearch 3 snippet]; "total kostnadsramme...ca 44,7 milliarder kroner (prisnivå desember 2024)" — [WebSearch 3 snippet, Sykehusbygg source]
**Reasoning:** The cost estimate increased from 42B to 44.7B NOK within approximately one year (Jan 2024 to Dec 2024), a ~6.5% increase before construction has fully mobilised. By triangulation with the NRK "over 50 billion" reference and the political controversy over the Gaustad decision, this trajectory suggests structural cost-escalation risk embedded in the programme's governance and scope definition.

**Inferred:** The OUS programme is being procured through multiple separate contract packages rather than a single integrated delivery model.
**Based on:** "Kontrakter innenfor bygg og teknikk for 25 milliarder kroner" (plural contracts) — [WebSearch 3 snippet]; "AF Gruppen vinner milliardkontrakt på råbygg" (separate structural package) — [WebSearch 3 snippet, Byggfakta Nyheter]
**Reasoning:** Norwegian public hospital procurement under Sykehusbygg HF's standard approach uses package-based contracting (separate structural, MEP, and specialist contracts) rather than single totalentreprise. The evidence consistently references plural packages rather than a single integrated contract.

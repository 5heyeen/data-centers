## 2. CEO Network Analysis — Potential Warm Investor Leads

### 2a. Valinor AS — **HIGH POTENTIAL**

**What:** Norwegian family-owned investment firm, est. 1996 (as Norsk Vind AS, rebranded 2019). Founded by **Lars Helge Helvig** (Norwegian wind power billionaire).

**Investment thesis:** Clean tech, renewable energy, infrastructure, aquaculture. NOK 2.5 billion value-adjusted capital base. 90% of profits reinvested in sustainable investments.

**Notable portfolio:**
- **Norsk Vind Energi AS** — Norway's largest private wind company (controlling interest)
- **Zaptec** — EV charging; developed and IPO'd at NOK 4.5B valuation
- **Norsk Havvind** — JV with TotalEnergies and Iberdrola on offshore wind
- Battery factory in Trøndelag (with former Morrow Batteries CEO)
- NOK 7B+ in renewable projects via Norsk Vind

**Vesterdal connection:** Held roles at Valinor (investment firm). This is a direct professional relationship.

**Fit with Fossefall:** STRONG — Valinor's thesis (clean energy infrastructure, Norwegian, family office patience, ~NOK 2.5B capital base) aligns perfectly with Fossefall's hydropower-to-AI strategy. Valinor could provide growth equity without PE-style exit pressure.

**Inference (MEDIUM confidence):** Valinor is a likely warm lead. Vesterdal worked there, and the thesis matches. However, Valinor has not publicly invested in data centers before — this would be a sector expansion for them.

Source: [Valinor.no](https://valinor.no/en/about-us/), [Valinor Portfolio](https://valinor.no/en/portfolio/), [MAInsights — Valinor battery](https://www.mainsights.io/ma-news/investment-firm-valinor-is-looking-for-investors-for-a-new-battery-factory)

### 2b. Glacier Link Capital AS — **MEDIUM POTENTIAL**

**What:** Norwegian investment consulting and capital management firm. Multiple entities:
- Glacier Link Investment Consulting AS
- Glacier Link Capital AS (Org. 928215253, incorporated Nov 2021, Oslo)
- Glacier Link Capital 2 AS (Org. 936250351, Oslo)

**Details:** 10-19 employees, $500K-$1M revenue (ZoomInfo). Business consulting focus. Address: Volvat terrasse 1, 0369 Oslo.

**Vesterdal connection:** Former partner at Glacier Link.

**Fit with Fossefall:** MODERATE — Glacier Link appears to be a smaller advisory/investment shop. Vesterdal's partnership there means warm relationships, but the firm likely doesn't have the capital scale for a major Fossefall round. More likely a **co-investment or advisory channel** to larger capital.

**Inference (LOW confidence):** Glacier Link's LPs and network connections could be more valuable than a direct investment. Vesterdal's partner relationships there may connect to larger institutional capital.

Sources: [ZoomInfo](https://www.zoominfo.com/c/glacier-link-investment-consulting/558006384), [Tracxn](https://tracxn.com/d/legal-entities/norway/glacier-link-capital-as/__HKAm7I-HbTLPLi7OmRlHMspoHhY5gitwY5_7j3jo8DI), [yra.no](https://yra.no/en/company/928215253/roles)

**What:** Norwegian energy company that planned to offtake up to 400MW of excess offshore wind power for data centers in Northern Europe.

**Key people:**
- **Ingvil Smines Tybring-Gjedde** — CEO & Co-Founder; former Norwegian Minister of National Public Security, Deputy Minister of Petroleum and Energy
- **Maryann Løcka** — Business Development Manager; 25+ years at Norwegian Ministry of Petroleum and Energy
- **Vesterdal** — Former CEO

**Status:** EWP appears to still be active. Presented at COP28.

**Fit with Fossefall:** EWP's investor/backer network could be relevant — a company planning 400MW of offshore wind to DC infrastructure would have attracted energy infrastructure investors. Vesterdal's former CEO role means direct access. The **government/policy connections** (former ministers) are also valuable for navigating Norwegian regulatory landscape.

**Inference (LOW confidence):** EWP's specific investors are not publicly disclosed. However, the political connections (Tybring-Gjedde, Løcka) could open doors to government-adjacent capital (Investinor, Nysnø, ENOVA).

Sources: [Offshore Wind](https://www.offshorewind.biz/2022/11/23/norwegian-company-to-power-data-centres-with-offshore-wind/), [DCD](https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/data-center-startup-fossefall-launches-in-norway-targets-500mw-of-ai-capacity/)

### 2d. AMD Ventures (via Seekr) — **MEDIUM POTENTIAL**

**What:** AMD's corporate venture arm. ~$1B capital. 71 investments. Invests in: Horizontal AI, Vertical AI, and **Data Center Infrastructure** (AI Clouds, Silicon Technologies, Interconnects).

**Seekr connection:** Seekr Technologies is in AMD Ventures' portfolio. Seekr holds 15.6% of Fossefall and is the anchor customer. This creates a warm intro path.

**Portfolio DC-relevant companies:** Ayar Labs, Celestial AI, Ranovus, TensorWave, Pliops, Bodo.ai

**Fit with Fossefall:** MODERATE — AMD Ventures invests in DC infrastructure, but their portfolio is heavily weighted toward silicon/component companies, not DC operators/developers. A direct equity investment in Fossefall would be unusual for their thesis. However, AMD could provide **strategic support** (hardware partnerships, customer introductions) that makes Fossefall more attractive to other investors.

**Inference (MEDIUM confidence):** More likely as a follow-on strategic investor or through expanding the Seekr relationship than as a lead investor in a capital raise.

Source: [AMD Ventures](https://www.amd.com/en/ventures.html), [AMD Ventures Portfolio](https://www.amd.com/en/ventures/portfolio.html)

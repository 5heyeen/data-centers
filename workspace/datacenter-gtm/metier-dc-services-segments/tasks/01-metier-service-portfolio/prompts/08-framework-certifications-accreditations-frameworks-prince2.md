# Prompt 08: framework-certifications-accreditations-frameworks-prince2

> **Prompt:** What certifications, accreditations, or frameworks (PRINCE2, PMI, ISO, etc.) are most valued by clients buying project management services? How do these signal capability?

---

## Certifications & Frameworks Valued in Capital-Intensive Project Management

### Process Frameworks

**PRINCE2** dominates in public sector and government-adjacent procurement across Scandinavia and the UK. Norwegian public agencies (Statsbygg, Nye Veier, Equinor government-facing units) frequently specify PRINCE2 in tender requirements. Signals structured escalation paths and documentation discipline.

**PMP (PMI)** carries more weight with international private sector clients, PE-backed infrastructure funds with Anglo-American GPs, and multinational energy companies. Signals individual practitioner competence and adherence to globally recognized standards.

### Quality & Management System Standards

**ISO 9001** (quality management) is essentially table-stakes for any serious PM consultancy. Signals auditable, repeatable internal processes — important for clients who must demonstrate governance to boards, lenders, and regulators.

**ISO 21500 / ISO 21502** are newer and referenced in European public procurement and by development finance institutions (EIB, NIB). Signals alignment with international project governance norms.

### Industry-Specific Frameworks

**NORSOK / IPA (Independent Project Analysis)** carry more practical weight in energy and offshore than generic PM certifications. Demonstrates capital efficiency credibility with sophisticated energy sector buyers.

**NS 8400-series contracts** (Norwegian Standard) matter more to public clients than individual certifications for construction/infrastructure procurement.

### Buyer Type Signal Map

| Buyer Type | Most Valued Signals |
|---|---|
| Norwegian public sector | PRINCE2, ISO 9001, NS-compliance, audit-ready documentation |
| PE-backed operators | PMP density, schedule/cost track record, IPA benchmarking, IRR-aligned reporting |
| International DC / hyperscale | PMP, ISO 9001, FIDIC experience, Uptime Institute methodology awareness |
| Energy majors (Equinor, Aker) | NORSOK fluency, Stage Gate expertise, HSE certification alignment |
| Development finance / public infra | ISO 21502, OPS experience, ESG documentation capability |

### Key Insight

For sophisticated buyers, certifications function as a **minimum filter, not a differentiator**. What actually differentiates is a demonstrated track record on analogous projects at comparable scale. Certifications answer "are they professional?" — references and case studies answer "can they handle our specific problem?"

## Section 1 — Definition and Structural Features of Norwegian Samspill

### What is samspill?

Norwegian "samspill" (literally: interplay/collaboration) is a project delivery model in which the client, contractor, and typically key consultants work jointly through a structured collaboration phase before the implementation contract is signed. The key structural features:

**Standard "totalentreprise med samhandling/samspill" (most common form):**
- Single total contractor responsible for design + construction + sub-contractors
- Preceded by a samhandlingsfase / samspillsfase (ECI collaboration phase) in which parties jointly optimise design and cost toward a target cost
- Implementation contract (gjennomføringskontrakt) signed after the collaboration phase, conditional on reaching the target cost
- Analogous to "IPD-lite" or single-party ECI alliance in international terminology

**Full IPL (Integrert Prosjektleveranse — Norwegian term for multi-party IPD):**
- Owner + contractor + consultant(s) in a shared multi-party agreement
- Shared risk/gain: all parties receive payment from a common cost pool
- Open budgets, integrated team, shared leadership structure
- Direct Norwegian equivalent of AIA's multi-party IPD agreement

"I IPL, inngår byggherren, entreprenøren og rådgiverne en felles trepartsavtale med delt risiko og gevinst, med alle nøkkelaktørene involvert fra starten, organisert som et integrert team med felles ledelsesstruktur, åpne budsjetter og felles mål." (In IPL, the owner, contractor, and consultants enter into a shared three-party agreement with shared risk and gain, with all key actors involved from the start, organized as an integrated team with shared leadership structure, open budgets, and shared objectives.) — [Search snippet referencing Nye Veier IPL documentation, WebSearch, 2026-04-25]

## Section 3 — Key Findings on Challenges at Scale

"Samspill krever mye av byggherreorganisasjonen når det gjelder kompetanse, bemanning og ressurser til oppfølgning, og er en relativt ressurskrevende gjennomføringsmodell for byggherreorganisasjonen. Samspill anses ikke som særlig hensiktsmessig i standardprosjekter, men prosjektene bør være av en viss størrelse og/eller kompleksitet." (Samspill requires a lot from the owner's organisation in terms of competence, staffing and resources, and is a relatively resource-intensive model for the owner's organisation. Samspill is not considered particularly appropriate for standard projects, but projects should be of a certain size and/or complexity.) — [Search snippet from Concept research / Marstrand, WebSearch, 2026-04-25]

"Kompetansen på byggherresiden er variabel og til dels begrenset, og samspillskontrakter utvikles ofte etter beste evne av folk som ikke har egen erfaring med gjennomføring av samspill." (The competence on the owner side is variable and partly limited, and samspill contracts are often developed to the best of the ability of people who have no own experience of executing samspill.) — [Search snippet from Concept research, WebSearch, 2026-04-25]

"Det er lite kildemateriale i Norge, og bransjen har i liten grad lykkes i å koordinere tiltak for å utvikle modeller. Det eksperimenteres med et utall varianter – gode og dårlige." (There is little source material in Norway, and the industry has largely failed to coordinate measures to develop models. A multitude of variants — good and bad — are being experimented with.) — [Search snippet from Concept/Marstrand research, WebSearch, 2026-04-25]

The Concept programme has published two dedicated reports on samspill: Rapport 61 (2020) on "Samspillprosjekter i bygg- og anleggsbransjen" and Rapport 74 (2023) on "Utfordringer i samspillprosjekter." — [Bygg og prosjektjus: https://www.byggogprosjektjus.no/en/artikkel/utfordringer-i-samspillprosjekter-concept-nr-74-des-2023]

## Section 4 — Statsbygg's Rationale for Choosing Samspill

Statsbygg chose totalentreprise med samspill for the Livsvitenskapsbygget (7B NOK) based on direct experience from Campus Ås and the new National Museum:

"Statsbygg vil kutte i prosjekteringsregningen — gir entreprenør mer ansvar for å unngå kostnadssprekk" (Statsbygg will cut design costs — gives contractor more responsibility to avoid cost overruns) — [Byggeindustrien: https://www.bygg.no/statsbygg-vil-kutte-i-prosjekteringsregningen-gir-entreprenor-mer-ansvar-for-a-unnga-kostnadssprekk/1362627!/]

"Erfaringer fra Campus Ås og det nye Nasjonalmuseet var grunnlaget for valget av totalentreprise med samspill på syv milliarder-prosjektet på Blindern. Statsbyggs direktør...ønsket å få mer kontroll over prosjekteringskostnadene i store byggeprosjekter" (Experiences from Campus Ås and the new National Museum were the basis for choosing totalentreprise with samspill on the seven billion project at Blindern. Statsbygg's director wanted to get more control over design costs in large building projects) — [Search snippet, WebSearch, 2026-04-25]

"Statsbygg har laget Samspillsboka" — Statsbygg has published a "Samspillsbok" (book about samspill) consolidating their experience — [https://www.statsbygg.no/nyheter/statsbygg-har-laget-samspillsboka]

## Section 5 — Hospital Sector Samspill: What Scale Has Been Reached?

Based on all evidence gathered:

**Largest confirmed Norwegian hospital samspill:** Sørlandet Sykehus at >2.2B NOK (procurement open, contract model not fully confirmed). — [Sykehusbygg HF press release]

**Confirmed hospital samspill with published details:** Hjelset akuttsykehus (Helse Møre og Romsdal + Skanska, "totalentreprise med samhandling", October 2022). — [Task 01 research, Sykehusbygg HF search snippet]

**Critical scale gap:** The largest confirmed Norwegian samspill/IPL contract in any sector is ~7B NOK (Livsvitenskapsbygget). The target hospital megaproject scale is ~20B NOK. No samspill contract anywhere in Norway — hospital or otherwise — has been executed at above ~7B NOK.

## Inferred Findings

**Inferred:** The demonstrated upper bound of Norwegian samspill practice (~7B NOK, single totalentreprise with ECI phase) is approximately 3x below the 20B NOK megaproject threshold — meaning scaling samspill to this level would require a structural model innovation, not just a larger application of the current approach.
**Based on:** "Statsbygg goes for samspill on Livsvitenskapsbygget...total budget approximately NOK 7 billion" — [Tungt.no/WebSearch snippet, 2026-04-25]; "4.5 billion kroner...totalentreprise with collaboration" — [Nye Veier/WebSearch snippet, 2026-04-25]
**Reasoning:** The largest Norwegian samspill contract (7B NOK) is a totalentreprise with ECI/collaboration phase — a single main contractor. At 20B NOK, this structure would put enormous risk on a single contractor and likely exceed the risk appetite and capacity of any single Norwegian construction firm. A multi-contractor or clustered alliance model would be needed. This is a structural break point, not a linear scale-up.

**Inferred:** Norwegian samspill practice is evolving toward full IPL (multi-party shared risk/gain) at small scale (500M NOK), with Statsbygg and Nye Veier as the leading public clients — suggesting the institutional knowledge is available but the model has not yet been applied at megaproject scale.
**Based on:** "carried out as an IPL-contract...tripartite collaboration between Veidekke, Norconsult, and Statsbygg" at ~500M NOK — [WebSearch snippet, Statsbygg NTNU P4 Musikk, 2026-04-25]; "Nye Veier AS was the first public client that developed contract terms for IPL from the ground up" — [WebSearch snippet, 2026-04-25]
**Reasoning:** Two major Norwegian public clients (Statsbygg and Nye Veier) have demonstrated genuine multi-party IPD/IPL contracts, establishing legal and governance precedent. The trajectory — from totalentreprise med samspill (1B NOK+ projects) → IPL (500M NOK pilot) — suggests the Norwegian market is building toward larger IPL applications, but has not yet made the leap to megaproject scale.

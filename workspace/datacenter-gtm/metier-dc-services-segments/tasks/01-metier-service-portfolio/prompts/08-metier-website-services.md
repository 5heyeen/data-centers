## Service Descriptions (from search snippets)

### 1. Ledelse og styring av fysiske prosjekter (Physical Projects)
Metier leads and manages Norway's largest projects across: healthcare buildings (sykehus), construction and facilities, energy and industry, and infrastructure and transport. Specific sub-services mentioned:
- Project management and control (prosjektstyring)
- Contract strategy and procurement (kontraktstrategi, anskaffelse)
- Early-phase advisory (tidligfaseråd)
- Supports construction clients (byggherrene) with HMS, organisation, stakeholders and contracts
- Progress, cost and quality control
- Risk management (risikostyring)
- 300+ completed projects, NOK 1,000+ billion in investments managed
- 40+ years of experience

### 2. Ledelse og styring av digitale initiativer (Digital Initiatives)
Metier Digital works end-to-end: from system procurement through to implementation completion. Services include:
- Digital transformation advisory
- IT system procurement
- Implementation and change management support

### 3. Kompetanseutvikling (Competence Development)
Training and courses in: project management, agile methods, leadership and team development. Includes:
- Bedriftstilpasset opplæring (corporate tailored training)
- Metier Academy (online/e-learning products)
- PRINCE2, PMI certifications implied

## Scale Indicators
- ~300 professionals (as of RPS integration 2015; likely grown since)
- Offices: Oslo, Stavanger, Trondheim, Ålesund
- Framework agreement with Sykehusbygg: NOK 140M (2025)
- Key sectors: healthcare, construction/property, energy/industry, infrastructure/transport

## Data Center References
**No data center or datasenter mentions found on public website.** All sector references are: healthcare, construction, energy, oil & gas, infrastructure.

## Key Observation
The public website organises services by delivery type (physical vs. digital vs. training), not by sector. This makes DC relevance invisible to a DC operator browsing the site. The DC-specific framing exists only in internal documentation.

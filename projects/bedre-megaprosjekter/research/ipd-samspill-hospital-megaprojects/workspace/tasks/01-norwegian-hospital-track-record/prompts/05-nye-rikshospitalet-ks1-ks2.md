## Research Summary

### 1. KS Framework for Health Enterprises

The Norwegian KS1/KS2 quality assurance scheme (Statens prosjektmodell) was established by the Ministry of Finance to provide external quality assurance of major public investment projects. However, its application to health enterprise projects (helseforetak) is structured differently from standard state projects.

"Ekstern kvalitetssikring av investeringsprosjekter i statlige foretak og [selskaper]" — [NTNU Concept Working Paper 2019-7: https://www.ntnu.no/documents/1261860271/1262021752/Arbeidsrapport+2019-7+Prosjektmodeller+foretak+og+as.pdf/9d58ea7e-d420-042e-680f-9e664042e25a — 403 on fetch]

Health enterprises (helseforetak) are state-owned enterprises (statsforetak), not ministries. The Riksrevisjonen has noted that they are subject to KS-equivalent external quality assurance processes, but the exact requirements and processes differ from the standard KS scheme applied to direct state investments.

### 2. External Quality Assurance for Aker/Gaustad/Rikshospitalet

A PwC external quality assurance (KSK — Konseptfase) was performed on the Aker/Gaustad concept in 2019:

"www.pwc.no Ekstern kvalitetssikring KSK Videreutvikling av Aker og Gaustad" — [https://www.helse-sorost.no/49a00b/siteassets/documents/store-utviklingsprosjekter/ous/aker-gaustad/tilleggsutredning-2019/ksk---konseptfase-barn-fodselshjelp-og-gynekologi---versjon-1.0---endelig.pdf — not fetched]

Additionally, an "Usikkerhetsanalyse Gaustad sykehus" (uncertainty/risk analysis for Gaustad) was performed by Helse Sør-Øst in November 2018 and is publicly available as a PDF — [https://www.helse-sorost.no/49a01c/siteassets/documents/store-utviklingsprosjekter/ous/aker-gaustad/konseptutredning-aker-og-gaustad/usikkerhetsanalyse-gaustad-sykehus.pdf — not fetched]

### 3. P50/P85 Cost Estimates from Forprosjekt (Design Development)

The most authoritative public cost figure for Nye Rikshospitalet/Nye Aker comes from the forprosjektrapport approved in 2022:

"To prosjektene er planlagt gjennomført innenfor en økonomisk styringsramme (P50-estimat) på 36 045 millioner kroner (prisnivå januar 2021), inkludert ikke-bygg-relatert IKT med inntil 1 545 millioner kroner. Justert til juni 2022-priser er prosjektenes kostnad 39 074 millioner kroner inkludert ikke-bygg-relatert IKT med inntil 1 653 millioner kroner." — [NTB pressemelding — Forprosjekt Nye Aker og Nye Rikshospitalet vedtatt: https://kommunikasjon.ntb.no/pressemelding/forprosjekt-nye-aker-og-nye-rikshospitalet-vedtatt?publisherId=15925840&releaseId=17950721]

No P85 estimate from this source was retrievable from search snippets. The NRK "over 50 billion" figure may approximate or exceed the P85 risk-adjusted estimate. — [NRK Stor-Oslo: https://www.nrk.no/stor-oslo/nye-oslo-sykehus-vedtatt---kan-koste-over-50-milliarder-1.16221614 — 403 on fetch]

### 4. Concept Research Programme — Cost Control Under KS2

The Concept programme (NTNU) has published research specifically on cost control in projects subject to external quality assurance:

"Kostnadskontroll i statlige prosjekter med og uten ekstern kvalitetssikring" (Cost control in public projects with and without external quality assurance) — [Concept/NTNU Rapport 1277: https://www.ntnu.no/documents/1261860271/1262021752/Rapport+1277_sluttrapport_endelig.pdf — 403 on fetch]

"Concept-programmet, NTNU Kostnadskontroll i prosjekter som har vært underlagt ekstern kvalitetssikring KS2" — [NTNU: https://www.ntnu.no/documents/1261860271/1262021752/Kostnadskontroll+i+prosjekter+som+har+v%C3%A6rt+underlagt+ekstern+kvalitetssikring+KS2.pdf — 403 on fetch]

From search snippet on Concept research: "Deficiencies in the early phase of projects, before the decision to initiate the project was made, was the overarching main cause of large cost overruns in project implementation." — [Concept report, WebSearch snippet, 2026-04-25]

### 5. Håndbok Veien gjennom KS2

The Project Norge (NSP) published a handbook: "Håndbok Veien gjennom KS2" — [https://www.prosjektnorge.no/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/NSP-0022-Veien-gjennom-KS-2-handbok.pdf — not fetched] — which describes how external quality assurance is structured at KS2 stage, but this could not be fetched to extract specific hospital project guidance.

### 6. Procurement Strategy Findings from KS

No KS report specifically recommending a samspill, IPD, or alliance contract model for Nye Rikshospitalet/Nye Aker was found in any accessible source. The 2019 PwC KSK covered the concept phase (which function goes to which site), not the construction procurement strategy. The forprosjektrapport would be the source for procurement strategy recommendations, but was not accessible (403).

## Inferred Findings

**Inferred:** The KS/KSK process for the OUS megaproject programme has not been effective at constraining cost escalation, as evidenced by the ~24% increase between the 2021 P50 and the December 2024 cost figure.
**Based on:** "P50-estimat på 36 045 millioner kroner (prisnivå januar 2021)" — [NTB pressemelding, https://kommunikasjon.ntb.no/pressemelding/forprosjekt-nye-aker-og-nye-rikshospitalet-vedtatt?publisherId=15925840&releaseId=17950721]; "ca 44,7 milliarder kroner (prisnivå desember 2024)" — [Helse Sør-Øst/Sykehusbygg source, WebSearch 2026-04-25]
**Reasoning:** The Concept programme research notes that deficiencies in the early phase are the main cause of cost overruns. A 24% increase in ~3 years, even accounting for genuine inflation, suggests that the KS/concept-phase quality assurance did not adequately resolve scope definition or risk. This pattern — where approved P50 estimates significantly understate final costs — is consistent with the "front-end quality" problem that the KS scheme was designed to address but has not fully solved for complex health projects.

## Unverified Observations

- The specific KS1/KS2 quality assurance for Nye Rikshospitalet/Nye Aker as part of Helse Sør-Øst's process is not straightforwardly documented in public sources. Health enterprises are not required to use the same KS scheme as ministries.
- The PwC KSK 2019 (concept phase) covered functional programme (what functions go to Aker vs. Gaustad), not cost and construction procurement strategy. A separate cost-focused external QA at forprosjekt stage may have been performed but was not found in accessible sources.
- The Concept programme report "Regime for planlegging og beslutning i sykehusprosjekter" (Rapport 25) is specifically about hospital project decision regimes and may contain recommendations about quality assurance processes, but was not fetched.

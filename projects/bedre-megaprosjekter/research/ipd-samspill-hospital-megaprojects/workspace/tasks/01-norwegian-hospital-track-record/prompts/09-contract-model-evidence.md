# Prompt 09: contract-model-evidence

> **Prompt:** Establish which contract models have been used for major Norwegian hospital projects, and whether the contract model has been cited as a factor in project outcomes. Distinguish single main contractor vs. split contracts, DB vs. DBB, and any ECI or relational contracting use.

## Research Summary

### 1. Sykehusbygg HF Contract Strategy Framework

Sykehusbygg HF (the national hospital building enterprise) has published guidance on contract strategy for hospital projects. The key finding from search results:

"Alle entreprisemodeller kan kombineres med samspillsfaser, hvor partene i fellesskap blant annet kan utføre kvalitetssikring, optimalisering av løsninger, identifisere kostnadsbesparende tiltak m.m." (All enterprise/contract models can be combined with samspill phases, where the parties jointly can conduct quality assurance, solution optimisation, identify cost-saving measures, etc.) — [Sykehusbygg HF Kunnskapsbanken — Kontraktstrategi: https://kunnskapsbanken.sykehusbygg.no/hvordan-planlegge-og-bygge-sykehus/tema-pa-tvers-av-fasene/i/LkxiM/kontraktstrategi — 403 on fetch; cited from search snippet, 2026-04-25]

"Ved valg av totalentreprise(r), skal forprosjektet resultere i en detaljert funksjonsbeskrivelse som benyttes som underlag for anskaffelse av totalentreprenør." (When choosing totalentreprise, the design development phase should result in a detailed functional specification used as basis for procuring the total contractor.) — [Sykehusbygg HF Kunnskapsbanken, WebSearch snippet, 2026-04-25]

"Ved valg av tidlig involvering av entreprenør, vil partene i forprosjektet utvikle prosjektet i fellesskap" (When choosing early contractor involvement, the parties will develop the project jointly in the design development phase) — [Sykehusbygg HF Kunnskapsbanken, WebSearch snippet, 2026-04-25]

This confirms that Sykehusbygg's framework explicitly includes ECI (Early Contractor Involvement) as an option, and allows samspill phases to be combined with any enterprise model.

### 2. Documented Use of Samspill in Norwegian Hospital Projects

**Case: Hjelset hospital — Helse Møre og Romsdal / Skanska**

"In October 2022, Helse Møre og Romsdal signed a contract with Skanska Norge AS regarding further development of the acute hospital at Hjelset. The goal in the collaboration phase was to reduce costs down to the budget target set for the project." — [Search snippet from Sykehusbygg HF search result, WebSearch, 2026-04-25]

This is a direct example of a Norwegian hospital project using a samspill/collaboration phase structure with a target cost objective. The model appears to be a "totalentreprise med samhandling" (total contract with collaboration/ECI phase):

"Totalentreprise med samhandling var kontraktmodellen hvor Skanska påtok seg ansvaret for totalentreprisen inkludert prosjektering, tekniske entreprenører, samt andre nødvendige entreprenører og leverandører. Etter samhandlingsfasen var planen å inngå gjennomføringskontrakt med Skanska høsten 2020." (Total contract with collaboration was the contract model where Skanska took on responsibility for the total contract including design, technical contractors, and other necessary contractors and suppliers. After the collaboration phase, the plan was to enter an implementation contract with Skanska in autumn 2020.) — [Search snippet, WebSearch, 2026-04-25]

**Key structural features of "totalentreprise med samhandling" as used in Norwegian hospital projects:**
- Single total contractor (Skanska in the Hjelset example) responsible for design + construction + sub-contractors
- Preceded by a samhandlingsfase (collaboration/ECI phase) where cost is worked down toward a target budget
- Implementation contract (gjennomføringskontrakt) signed after the samhandlingsfase — conditional on reaching the target cost

## Inferred Findings

**Inferred:** Norwegian hospital projects have adopted "totalentreprise med samhandling" (total contract with collaboration/ECI phase) as the leading collaborative contract form — but this falls short of full IPD or project alliance. It uses a single total contractor rather than a multi-party risk-sharing structure.
**Based on:** "Totalentreprise med samhandling var kontraktmodellen...etter samhandlingsfasen var planen å inngå gjennomføringskontrakt" — [Sykehusbygg search snippet, 2026-04-25]; "Alle entreprisemodeller kan kombineres med samspillsfaser" — [Sykehusbygg Kunnskapsbanken, WebSearch snippet, 2026-04-25]
**Reasoning:** The described model — single total contractor + ECI collaboration phase + conditional implementation contract on reaching target cost — is structurally similar to "IPD-lite" or a single-party alliance, but does not involve multi-party risk pooling or integrated insurance. It is a step toward collaborative contracting but retains the single-contractor structure of totalentreprise rather than the multi-party shared risk of full IPD.

**Inferred:** Contract model has likely been a contributing factor in Norwegian hospital project outcomes, but this has not been explicitly documented in accessible official reviews.
**Based on:** "Varslet katastrofe" (predicted catastrophe) regarding conflicts at SUS2023 — [NRK Rogaland, WebSearch snippet, 2026-04-25]; "Alle entreprisemodeller kan kombineres med samspillsfaser" — [Sykehusbygg Kunnskapsbanken, implying traditional models without samspill are recognised as suboptimal, WebSearch snippet, 2026-04-25]
**Reasoning:** The absence of confirmed contract model data for most major Norwegian hospital projects (Ahus, OUS, SUS) in official post-project reviews, combined with Sykehusbygg's own guidance recommending samspill phases as an enhancement to all contract types, suggests the industry recognises traditional models as insufficient but has not formally attributed specific overruns to contract model failure.

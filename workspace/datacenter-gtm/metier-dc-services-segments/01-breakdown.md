# Breakdown: Metier DC Services & Target Segments

**Date:** 2026-04-18

## Context
Research what Metier currently offers, how each service applies to data center operators, and which DC operator segments should be prioritised as targets. The key risk is a gap between Metier's public website and their actual capabilities — internal docs should be cross-referenced throughout. Deliverable is an internal strategy brief for the Datacenter GTM effort.

## Subtasks

1. **Catalogue Metier's full service portfolio** [independent] — Document all service lines from metier.no + internal docs to establish the complete capability picture
2. **Map each service to data center operator needs** [independent] — For each service, define which DC pain points it addresses, how it would be framed, and who would buy it
3. **Profile data center operator types and their needs** [independent] — Analyse DC operator archetypes (hyperscalers, colo providers, high-security, enterprise DC, edge) to understand project challenges, procurement behaviour, and service needs — framed as potential Metier clients
4. **Identify expansion opportunities** [independent] — What DC-specific needs exist that current services don't fully cover? What could Metier adapt or add?
5. **Define and prioritize target segments with rationale** [sequential — depends on 2+3] — Match operator profiles to Metier's strongest services, rank segments by fit and opportunity, provide clear targeting rationale

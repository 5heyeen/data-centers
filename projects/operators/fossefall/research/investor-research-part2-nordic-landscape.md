### Patterns from Comparable Deals

1. **Valuations are surging.** atNorth went from Partners Group acquisition (~$300-400M inferred) to $4B sale in ~4 years. Green Mountain: $850M in 2022. GlobalConnect targeting €8B. The market rewards scale + renewables + AI positioning.

2. **Infrastructure PE dominates.** The biggest Nordic DC deals are all infrastructure PE: Partners Group, Ardian, BGO, EQT. These firms have the capital scale and hold periods for DC platform plays.

3. **Pension funds are entering directly.** CPP Investments taking 60% of atNorth for $1.6B signals that pension funds now view Nordic DCs as core infrastructure, not alternative investments.

4. **Brownfield conversion is validated.** Bitdeer converting a crypto mine to 180MW AI DC in Tydal is directly analogous to Fossefall's brownfield strategy. This de-risks Fossefall's model for investors.

5. **Entry at earlier stages is happening.** Nscale raised $1.1B Series B before Stargate Norway was operational. atNorth received Partners Group backing when it was just 83MW. Investors are comfortable backing development-stage Nordic DC platforms.

6. **Norwegian hydropower is a premium.** Every major deal cites renewable energy as a core thesis. Norway's ~0.009 USD/kWh hydropower is a structural advantage that attracts ESG-conscious capital.

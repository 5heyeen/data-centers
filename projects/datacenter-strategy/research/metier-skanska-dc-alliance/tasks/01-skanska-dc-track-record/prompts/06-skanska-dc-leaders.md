## 2. Recommendation — Best First Point of Contact for Metier Alliance Conversation

**Primary: Anders Geirsta, EVP Civil Construction, Skanska Norge.**

Reasoning:
1. **Geography fit** — Metier's core alliance leverage is Norwegian DC projects; Geirsta owns delivery of the NOK ~1.7bn Gromstul hyperscale, Skanska's largest publicly disclosed Nordic DC engagement (Skanska press releases above).
2. **Seniority** — EVP/Konserndirektør level is senior enough to authorize an alliance framework, but operationally closer to project reality than Ståle Rød (group-level EVP).
3. **Public engagement posture** — Geirsta has publicly endorsed third-party Norwegian construction-tech partners (Leasi), signaling openness to external alliance narratives.

**Escalation / parallel track: Ståle Rød (Group EVP)** — for an umbrella Nordic/UK alliance framing, given his remit spans Skanska Norway + UK + Central Europe.

**US bridge (if scope broadens): Katie Coulson (EVP/GM, Skanska Advanced Technology)** — owns the US DC delivery machine and is publicly vocal (Authority Magazine interview, September 2025 realignment announcement), making her the natural transatlantic reference for best-practice transfer into Nordics.

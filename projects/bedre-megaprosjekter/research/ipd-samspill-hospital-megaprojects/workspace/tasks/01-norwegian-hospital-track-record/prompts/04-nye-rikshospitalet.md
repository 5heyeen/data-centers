# Prompt 04: nye-rikshospitalet

> **Prompt:** Research the "Nye Rikshospitalet" project — Norway's planned new national hospital. Document current stage, estimated cost, planned completion, proposed contract/procurement strategy, and any controversies or cost estimate changes.

---

## Research Summary

### 1. Current Project Stage

Nye Rikshospitalet is one of two major components of the Oslo hospital megaproject, alongside Nye Aker. Both projects received board approval from Helse Sør-Øst RHF in December 2022 to proceed to implementation phase.

"Helse Sør-Øst vedtok Nye Rikshospitalet og Nye Aker" in December 2022, with the new hospitals potentially ready by 2031 if state regulation is approved. — [Uniforum, December 2022: https://www.uniforum.uio.no/nyheter/2022/12/helse-sor-ost-vedtok-nye-rikshospitalet-og-nye-ake.html — 403 on WebFetch; cited from search snippet]

A state planning regulation (statlig reguleringsplan) was requested through Regjeringen.no: "Nye Rikshospitalet og Nye Aker ved Oslo universitetssykehus HF — anmodning om gjennomføring av statlig reguleringsplan" — [Regjeringen.no: https://www.regjeringen.no/no/dokumenter/nye-rikshospitalet-og-nye-aker-ved-oslo-universitetssykehus-hf-anmodning-om-gjennomforing-av-statlig-reguleringsplan/id2906868/]

As of 2025/2026, the projects are in early construction / active procurement phase. AF Gruppen has won a råbygg (structural works) package at Rikshospitalet. — [Byggfakta Nyheter: https://byggfaktanyheter.no/af-gruppen-vinner-milliardkontrakt-pa-rabygg-ved-nye-rikshospitalet/ — 403 on WebFetch; cited from search snippet]

### 2. Estimated Total Investment Cost

The forprosjektrapport (detailed design report) for the two projects established the following cost framework:

"To prosjektene er planlagt gjennomført innenfor en økonomisk styringsramme (P50-estimat) på 36 045 millioner kroner (prisnivå januar 2021), inkludert ikke-bygg-relatert IKT med inntil 1 545 millioner kroner. Justert til juni 2022-priser er prosjektenes kostnad 39 074 millioner kroner inkludert ikke-bygg-relatert IKT med inntil 1 653 millioner kroner." — [NTB pressemelding — Forprosjekt Nye Aker og Nye Rikshospitalet vedtatt: https://kommunikasjon.ntb.no/pressemelding/forprosjekt-nye-aker-og-nye-rikshospitalet-vedtatt?publisherId=15925840&releaseId=17950721]

The more recent figure (December 2024 price level): "Total kostnadsramme for begge utbyggingsprosjektene er ca 44,7 milliarder kroner (prisnivå desember 2024)" — [Helse Sør-Øst / Sykehusbygg source, WebSearch, 2026-04-25]

Rikshospitalet (Gaustad) component specifically: "tilsvarende kostnad på Gaustad er 19 249 MNOK inkludert O-IKT og byggelånsrenter" — [WebSearch snippet, 2026-04-25]

### 3. Planned Completion Date

Planned clinical opening: 2031/32 — conditional on state planning regulation being approved promptly. This timeline has already been noted as tight given the permitting requirements.

### 4. Contract and Procurement Strategy

The evidence indicates a package-based (split-contract) procurement strategy rather than a single totalentreprise:

"Nye Aker og Nye Rikshospitalet skal inngå kontrakter innenfor bygg og teknikk for 25 milliarder kroner. Det skal dessuten anskaffes utstyr for de to sykehusene for om lag to milliarder" — [Sykehusbygg HF informasjonsmøte page, WebSearch, 2026-04-25]

The reference to plural "kontrakter" (contracts) for bygg og teknikk (building and technical/MEP) and a separate utstyr (equipment) procurement confirms a multi-package approach. The AF Gruppen råbygg contract confirms structural works are being let as a separate package. No source reviewed describes an IPD, alliance, or samspill model being used for Nye Rikshospitalet/Nye Aker.

### 5. Controversies and Cost Estimate Changes

**Major cost escalation documented:**
- January 2021 (P50): NOK 36,045M (combined Aker + Rikshospitalet)
- June 2022 adjustment: NOK 39,074M (+8.4%)
- January 2024: NOK 42,000M (approximately, as reported)
- December 2024: NOK 44,700M (+6.5% from Jan 2024)
- NRK projected: "kan koste over 50 milliarder" — [NRK Stor-Oslo: https://www.nrk.no/stor-oslo/nye-oslo-sykehus-vedtatt---kan-koste-over-50-milliarder-1.16221614 — 403 on fetch]

This represents a ~24% increase from the January 2021 P50 estimate to the December 2024 figure, before construction is substantially complete — suggesting material scope/cost control challenges.

**Site choice controversy:** The decision to build the new regional hospital at Gaustad rather than redevelop Ullevål was highly contested. One source reports: "Nytt sykehus på Gaustad kan bli 20 mrd. dyrere enn Ullevål" — [NAB: https://nab.no/nytt-sykehus-pa-gaustad-kan-bli-20-mrd-dyrere-enn-ulleval/19.18125]

A 2023 academic/policy critique in Samfunn og økonomi was titled "Statens sykehusutbygging i Oslo — Hvordan er det mulig å treffe så dårlige beslutninger?" (How is it possible to make such poor decisions?) — [https://www.samfunnogokonomi.no/2023/08/ — 403 on WebFetch]

## Inferred Findings

**Inferred:** The OUS megaproject programme has experienced a structural cost escalation of ~24% between the 2021 forprosjekt P50 estimate and the December 2024 figure, and is likely to continue escalating toward the NRK "50 billion" level by completion in 2031/32.
**Based on:** "P50-estimat på 36 045 millioner kroner (prisnivå januar 2021)" — [NTB pressemelding, https://kommunikasjon.ntb.no/pressemelding/forprosjekt-nye-aker-og-nye-rikshospitalet-vedtatt?publisherId=15925840&releaseId=17950721]; "ca 44,7 milliarder kroner (prisnivå desember 2024)" — [Helse Sør-Øst/Sykehusbygg source, WebSearch 2026-04-25]
**Reasoning:** The 24% increase over ~3 years represents both price-level adjustment and real scope/cost growth. Norwegian hospital megaprojects historically continue escalating through construction phases, and this project had already experienced political controversy over site selection before design began, suggesting the cost trajectory will continue upward.

## Unverified Observations

- The Forprosjektrapport for Nye Aker og Nye Rikshospitalet (PDF: https://www.helse-sorost.no/49a00c/siteassets/...) was referenced but returned 403 on fetch. This document likely contains the most detailed cost breakdown and risk assessment for the programme.
- No procurement strategy document describing a samspill/IPD/alliance model for these projects was found. All evidence points to traditional split-contract procurement.
- The "20 mrd. dyrere enn Ullevål" figure from NAB could not be verified from the page body and may reflect worst-case scenario analysis rather than a central estimate.

# Prompt 05: framework-main-categories-project-management-strategy

> **Prompt:** What are the main categories of services a project management and strategy consultancy typically offers? List the standard taxonomy (e.g., strategy, PMO, owner's engineer, training, digital, procurement).

---

## Standard Service Taxonomy for a Project Management & Strategy Consultancy

### 1. Strategy & Advisory
High-level counsel to leadership on direction, priorities, and investment decisions. Includes portfolio strategy, feasibility assessments, market entry analysis, business case development, and organizational capability assessments.

### 2. Program & Project Management (PMO Services)
The core delivery discipline. Encompasses establishing and running PMOs, defining governance frameworks, managing schedules and budgets, coordinating workstreams, risk management, and reporting to steering committees. Ranges from setting up a PMO structure to embedded project directors leading delivery.

### 3. Owner's Engineer / Owner's Representative
Acting on behalf of the "owner" in capital projects — representing the owner's interests toward contractors, engineers, and suppliers. Responsibilities include design review, contractor selection support, progress monitoring, quality assurance, change order management, and commissioning oversight.

### 4. Procurement & Contract Management
Supporting sourcing strategy, tender preparation, supplier evaluation, contract negotiation, and ongoing contract administration.

### 5. Planning & Scheduling
Specialist discipline: developing baseline schedules (Primavera P6, MS Project), critical path analysis, earned value management (EVM), delay analysis, and recovery planning.

### 6. Risk Management
Identifying, quantifying, and mitigating project and organizational risks. Includes qualitative risk workshops, quantitative Monte Carlo analysis, risk register maintenance, and insurance/contracting strategy advice.

### 7. Digital & Technology
Helping organizations adopt digital tools for project control: PMIS implementation, dashboards, BIM integration, document control systems, and data analytics for portfolio visibility.

### 8. Organizational Development & Change Management
Stakeholder engagement plans, communication strategies, organizational design, capability building, and managing resistance to change.

### 9. Training & Competency Development
Structured learning programs in PM methodologies (PMI/PMP, PRINCE2, Agile/hybrid), leadership, procurement, and scheduling. Linked to certification pathways.

### 10. Sustainability & ESG Integration
Embedding ESG criteria into project governance, sustainability reporting for capital projects, green procurement frameworks, carbon accounting, and alignment with EU Taxonomy or GRI standards.

### 11. Portfolio Management & Governance
Enterprise-level oversight of multiple projects: portfolio prioritization, resource allocation, benefits realization tracking, and governance design for boards and investment committees.

### How These Relate
Owner's Engineer, PMO, and Procurement are the highest-revenue categories in capital-intensive sectors. Training and Digital tend to be margin-enhancing add-ons that deepen client relationships.

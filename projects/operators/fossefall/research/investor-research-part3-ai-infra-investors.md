### Key Insight: Norwegian Public Capital Opportunity

Fossefall's thesis — converting cheap Norwegian hydropower into AI infrastructure, creating local jobs in rural municipalities — is politically resonant. The government has committed NOK 1B+ to AI over 5 years. Nysnø (NOK 5.4B AUM, state-owned, clean energy mandate) is the most natural public capital partner. EIB/NIB could provide project-level debt alongside equity investors.

# Prompt 06: mjossykehuset

> **Prompt:** Research Mjøssykehuset — the planned new main hospital for Sykehuset Innlandet. Document: project stage, location, estimated cost, planned timeline, contract/procurement strategy, and any cost escalation or controversy.

---

## Research Summary

### 1. Project Stage and Location

Mjøssykehuset is the planned new main hospital for Sykehuset Innlandet health trust, consolidating specialist health services for the Innlandet region (covering roughly 400,000 inhabitants across Hedmark and Oppland counties).

Location: Moelv, with the hospital site (sykehustomt) located in Moskogen, west of Moelv town centre. — [Sykehuset Innlandet: https://www.sykehuset-innlandet.no/nyheter/forslag-til-reguleringsplan-for-mjossykehuset/ — cited from search snippet, 2026-04-25]

As of 2026, the project is in detailed planning/regulatory approval phase, with a forslag til reguleringsplan (proposed zoning plan) having been submitted. — [Sykehuset Innlandet, WebSearch 2, 2026-04-25]

"Prosjektet for videreutvikling av Sykehuset Innlandet trenger mer tid" (The project needs more time) — [Helse Sør-Øst: https://www.helse-sorost.no/nyheter/prosjektet-for-videreutvikling-av-sykehuset-innlandet-trenger-mer-tid/ — cited from search snippet, 2026-04-25] — indicating schedule delays have already occurred in the planning phase.

### 2. Estimated Total Project Cost

Government loan framework approved: "Regjeringen foreslår en samlet låneramme på 18 009 millioner kroner til nytt Mjøssykehus for Sykehuset Innlandet" — [NRK Innlandet: https://www.nrk.no/innlandet/regjeringa-setter-av-penger-til-nytt-sykehus-i-innlandet-i-statsbudsjettet-1.17511786 — cited from search snippet, 2026-04-25]

More specifically: "Regjeringen har satt av mellom 18 og 19 milliarder kroner til Sykehuset Innlandet, der 18 milliarder går til nytt sjukehusbygg og utstyr" — [WebSearch 2 snippet, 2026-04-25]

Note: The loan is structured as state loans to Helse Sør-Øst/Sykehuset Innlandet HF, not as a direct state grant, meaning the health trust carries the debt service burden.

### 3. Planned Completion Timeline

"Byggestart er planlagt i 2028 og ferdigstillelse av Mjøssykehuset i 2032-2033" — [WebSearch 2 snippet, 2026-04-25]

The project has already experienced delays — Helse Sør-Øst announced in 2024 that the project "trenger mer tid" (needs more time). — [https://www.helse-sorost.no/nyheter/prosjektet-for-videreutvikling-av-sykehuset-innlandet-trenger-mer-tid/]

"Mjøssykehuset på statsbudsjettet" — indicating the project was included in the state budget, suggesting political approval secured — [Sykehuset Innlandet: https://www.sykehuset-innlandet.no/nyheter/mjossykehuset-pa-statsbudsjettet/ — cited from search snippet, 2026-04-25]

### 4. Proposed Contract/Procurement Strategy

No specific contract or procurement strategy for Mjøssykehuset was found in publicly accessible sources during this research session. The Sykehusbygg 2023 guide for early-phase hospital planning was referenced but not accessible.

**Inferred:** Given that Mjøssykehuset is being developed by Sykehuset Innlandet HF with Sykehusbygg HF as builder-owner (byggherreorganisasjon), it will likely follow Sykehusbygg's standard contract strategy framework, which in recent projects has included "totalentreprise med samhandling" (total contract with collaboration phase) as an option. See Prompt 09 for Sykehusbygg contract strategy details.

### 5. Scope of Services

The new hospital will consolidate: "spesialiserte funksjoner innen somatikk, psykisk helsevern og tverrfaglig spesialisert rusbehandling på Mjøssykehuset, lokalisert til Moelv, etablering av en luftambulansebase i Elverum, elektiv ortopedi og rehabilitering, akutt indremedisin, fødestue og barselavdeling ved sykehuset i Elverum, og en styrking av tilbudet ved sykehuset på Tynset." — [WebSearch 2 snippet, 2026-04-25]

### 6. Cost Escalation and Controversy

The long site selection process for Mjøssykehuset was politically contentious. A Sykehuset Innlandet timeline page documents the extended history: — [https://www.sykehuset-innlandet.no/framtidig-sykehusstruktur/tidslinje]

The project has already experienced planning delays ("trenger mer tid"). At ~18-19B NOK, Mjøssykehuset is at the megaproject scale relevant to this research (~20B NOK threshold). No confirmed cost overrun data is available since the project has not yet entered construction phase.

Norwegian hospital debt burden context: NRK reported "Gjeldskrise for norske sykehus: Betaler milliarder i renter til staten" (Debt crisis for Norwegian hospitals: paying billions in interest to the state) — [NRK Rogaland: https://www.nrk.no/rogaland/gjeldskrise-for-norske-sykehus_-betaler-milliarder-i-renter-til-staten-1.17542482] — This structural financing constraint limits the hospitals' ability to absorb cost overruns.

## Inferred Findings

**Inferred:** Mjøssykehuset at ~18-19B NOK represents a project at the threshold scale of this research (target: ~20B NOK), making it the most directly relevant Norwegian comparator for assessing whether an IPD/samspill model could be applied at megaproject scale in Norway.
**Based on:** "samlet låneramme på 18 009 millioner kroner" — [NRK Innlandet, WebSearch 2, 2026-04-25]; "Regjeringen har satt av mellom 18 og 19 milliarder kroner" — [WebSearch 2 snippet, 2026-04-25]
**Reasoning:** The project has not yet selected a procurement/contract strategy (still in planning/regulatory phase as of 2026), creating an opportunity window to influence contract model selection before it is locked in at forprosjekt stage. This makes it the highest-priority Norwegian project for IPD/samspill feasibility assessment.

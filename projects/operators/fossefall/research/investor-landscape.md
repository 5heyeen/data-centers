## Executive Summary

Fossefall AS is well-positioned to attract institutional capital for its 500MW Norwegian AI infrastructure ambition. The current investor base (Seekr 15.6%, Stian Rustad, Ravenger Establishment, Done.ai 2.4%, executive team) is early-stage and strategic. To scale from ~18MW operational to 400+ MW, Fossefall needs €50-200M+ in growth equity, likely complemented by project debt.

**27 potential investors** were identified and ranked across 3 tiers. **8 Tier 1 investors** represent the most likely capital sources, led by:

1. **Valinor** (Lars Helge Helvig's Norwegian family office, NOK 2.5B) — Vesterdal's former employer, perfect thesis alignment
2. **Ardian** — Owns Verne Global, $1.2B committed to Nordic DC expansion
3. **BGO** — €640M+ into Bulk Infrastructure, deep Norwegian DC experience
4. **Partners Group** — Just exited atNorth at $4B, looking for next Nordic DC platform
5. **Nysnø Klimainvesteringer** — Norwegian state climate investor, NOK 5.4B, thesis-aligned

The Nordic DC investment market is booming — $20B+ deployed in 2024-2026 across deals including atNorth ($4B), Stargate Norway ($1B+), Brookfield Sweden ($10B), and CoreWeave Nordic ($2.2B). Fossefall's brownfield strategy is validated by Bitdeer's 180MW Tydal conversion and Bulk's campus model.

## Implications for Metier's Pitch

The investor landscape analysis reveals that **the type of investor Fossefall attracts will directly determine Metier's service opportunity**:

| If Fossefall raises from... | Metier's primary opportunity |
|---|---|
| Infrastructure PE (Ardian, Partners Group, EQT) | Owner's Engineer + Portfolio Governance — PE boards need independent CAPEX validation and milestone reporting to release capital tranches |
| RE investor (BGO, Azrieli) | Contract & Procurement + Project Execution — cost certainty and on-time delivery are paramount |
| Family office (Valinor) | Strategy Advisory + Early-Phase Advisory — patient capital wants the strategy right before scaling |
| State capital (Nysnø, Investinor) | Strategy Advisory + Portfolio Governance — ESG compliance and transparent reporting |
| Pension fund (CPP) | Portfolio Governance + Owner's Engineer — highest governance standards required |

**Key insight for the meeting:** Fossefall is currently pre-institutional capital. Metier can position as "the execution credibility that unlocks your next capital raise" — professional PM and governance frameworks are what institutional investors demand before writing checks.
